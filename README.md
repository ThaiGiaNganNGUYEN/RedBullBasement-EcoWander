# Red Bull Basement 2026 in Australia
<h1>EcoWander</h1>
<h3> The Challenge: </h3>
<p>
  Eco-conscious travellers must piece together carbon calculators, certification databases, and review sites across separate tools for every booking — resulting in guilt, burnout, or avoidance. <br>
  No platform independently verifies eco-credentials and surfaces them at the exact moment of booking. Travellers who care about their footprint have no reliable, unified tool to act on that care.
</p>

<h3> Our Solution: </h3>
<p>
  EcoWander lets eco-conscious travellers plan and book verified, sustainability-rated trips — flights, stays, restaurants, and experiences — in one place, in minutes. Every option carries a live eco-score so users know their impact before they book, not after.
</p>

<h3> The Tech: </h3>
<ul>
  <li>Platform: A single-page web app built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no framework required, deployable on any host.</li>
  <li>AI Planner:	A 4-step guided form that feeds user preferences into a large language model (Claude) to generate a personalised eco-rated itinerary with carbon savings and rewards estimates.</li>
  <li>Eco Rating:	A 6-dimension scoring model (carbon, biodiversity, water, community, waste, local economy) aggregated into an A–D grade shown on every destination, flight, stay, and activity.</li>
  <li>Chatbot:	An in-page AI assistant for quick review summaries, destination facts, and carbon comparisons — extensible to a live API for real-time responses.</li>
  <li>Rewards:	A points-based loyalty system with four tiers (Seed → Sprout → Canopy → Legend) that rewards eco-friendly bookings, reviews, and low-carbon choices with redeemable discounts.</li>
</ul>

<h3>The Impact: </h3>
<ul>
  <li>What changes:	Users go from 3–5 hours of fragmented research across Skyscanner, TripAdvisor, and carbon calculators to a complete, verified, eco-scored trip plan in under 10 minutes.</li>
  <li>Behaviour shift:	Rewards and tier progression turn one-off green choices into long-term habits — every booking decision becomes a visible step toward a lower-carbon travel identity.</li>
  <li>How we measure:	Average eco-score of booked trips, total CO₂ saved per user cohort, rewards redemption rate, repeat booking rate, and Net Promoter Score tracked monthly.</li>

</ul>
